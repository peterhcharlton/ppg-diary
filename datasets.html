Summary

We recently completed the PPG Diary Pilot 1 Study, an observational study to assess the feasibility of acquiring PPG data during daily living. The data acquired during this study consist of PPG pulse waves acquired from a single subject for a period of 28 days during daily living. We are currently collating and analysing these data, and intend to make them publicly available in due course.

Whilst this process is ongoing, we have prepared some preliminary subsets of the larger dataset, which are now publicly available.

Preliminary Subsets

The following preliminary subsets are currently available in Matlab ® format:

  • Preliminary Pulse Wave Database: Simulated arterial pulse waves from 258 virtual subjects aged from 25 to 75 years old. This is a preliminary version of the dataset, and cannot be considered to be representative of a sample of healthy adults. Nonetheless, it has proved helpful for developing pulse wave analysis algorithms and for gaining insight into arterial haemodynamics.
  • Mental Stress Pulse Wave Database: Simulated arterial pulse waves from virtual subjects simulated to mimic different levels of mental stress.
